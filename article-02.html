Top 10 Laptops of 2022

Published on November 15, 2025 • 8 min read

Our best picks for various needs and budgets

Whether you're a creative professional, a gamer, or a student, finding the right laptop can be overwhelming. We've tested dozens of models to bring you our top recommendations across different categories and price points.

Best Overall: MacBook Air M2

Apple's M2 chip delivers exceptional performance with outstanding battery life. The fanless design keeps it silent, while the stunning Retina display makes it perfect for creative work. Starting at $1,199, it offers incredible value for most users.

Best for Gaming: ASUS ROG Zephyrus G14

Compact yet powerful, the G14 packs AMD's Ryzen 9 processor and NVIDIA's RTX graphics into a surprisingly portable chassis. The 14-inch QHD display with 120Hz refresh rate delivers smooth, vibrant visuals for gaming and content creation.

Best Budget: Lenovo IdeaPad 3

At under $500, the IdeaPad 3 offers solid performance for everyday tasks. With a Ryzen 5 processor and 8GB of RAM, it handles web browsing, office work, and streaming with ease. The 15.6-inch Full HD display is crisp and bright.

Best for Business: Dell Latitude 9430

Enterprise-grade security features, all-day battery life, and a stunning 14-inch display make this the go-to choice for professionals. The premium build quality and comprehensive warranty support justify the higher price tag.

Best 2-in-1: Microsoft Surface Laptop Studio

The innovative hinge design allows seamless transitions between laptop, tablet, and studio modes. Combined with the Surface Pen support and powerful internals, it's ideal for designers and digital artists.

Best for Content Creators: Dell XPS 15

The 15.6-inch OLED display with 100% DCI-P3 color gamut is breathtaking. Paired with Intel's latest processors and NVIDIA graphics, it handles 4K video editing and 3D rendering without breaking a sweat.

Best Ultraportable: LG Gram 17

Incredibly, this 17-inch laptop weighs just 2.98 pounds. Despite its large screen, it's easy to carry all day. The battery lasts over 19 hours in our tests, making it perfect for frequent travelers.

Best Chromebook: Framework Laptop Chromebook Edition

Modular, repairable, and sustainable - this Chromebook represents the future of consumer electronics. Upgrade components as needed and customize ports to your liking, all while running Chrome OS.

Best for Students: HP Pavilion 14

Balanced performance, good battery life, and an affordable price make this an excellent choice for students. The 14-inch form factor is perfect for both dorm rooms and lecture halls.

Best Premium: Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Carbon Gen 10

The legendary ThinkPad keyboard, combined with modern specs and a carbon fiber chassis, creates the ultimate business laptop. It's expensive, but worth every penny for those who demand the best.
